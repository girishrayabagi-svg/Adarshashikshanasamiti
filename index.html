Admissions

Start Your Academic Journey

Simple, transparent, student-friendly. Your future begins with a single step.

How to Apply — Step by Step
1
Step 01
Check Eligibility
Verify that you meet the minimum qualification criteria for your chosen program — BBA or BCA.
2
Step 02
Fill Application
Complete the online application via the UUCMS portal or visit our admission desk in person.
3
Step 03
Submit Documents
Provide your 10th & 12th mark sheets, TC, Aadhaar, and passport-size photos at the office.
4
Step 04
Confirm & Join!
Complete the fee payment, get your confirmation letter, and welcome to the Potnis family!
Eligibility Criteria
BBA — Business Administration
10+2 (any stream) from a recognized board with minimum 45% marks
BCA — Computer Applications
10+2 with Mathematics / Computer Science preferred; minimum 45% marks
SC / ST Relaxation
Eligible for relaxation as per Karnataka state norms. Contact office for details.
